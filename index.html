Projects

"Brand Identity for a Startup"

  • In this project, I created a brand identity for a newly established tech startup. The project included the design of the logo, color scheme, typography, and a brand guidelines document. After several ideation sessions with the client, I designed a logo that perfectly represents their innovative and forward-thinking approach. The color scheme and typography were carefully selected to reflect the startup's identity. The brand guidelines document I provided will help them maintain consistency across all their future branding endeavors.

"Interactive Non-profit Organization Website"

  • For this non-profit organization, I designed and developed a website with a focus on storytelling and interactivity to increase audience engagement. I used a combination of dynamic infographics, videos, and other multimedia elements to convey the organization's mission and impact. The website also includes a secure donation platform, implemented using a combination of JavaScript and secure third-party payment APIs. The design's success was reflected in a 50% increase in site traffic and a 40% boost in donations.

"Mobile-First Travel Blog"

  • I developed a responsive, mobile-first travel blog for a professional travel blogger. Understanding that most of the blogger's audience would be accessing the site from mobile devices, I prioritized a design that would look great and perform well on smaller screens. I also integrated a CMS using WordPress, allowing the blogger to easily update their site with new travel stories and photos. The project increased the blogger's audience by 35%.
